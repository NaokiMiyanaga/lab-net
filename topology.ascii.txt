  (No direct L2 link between L2A and L2B; each VLAN is an independent segment)

  Notes
  - mgmt-plane (mgmtnet) carries Router-ID and SNMP; SNMP ports are not host-published in dual-plane overlay.
  - service-plane (labnet) is used for BGP peering: R1 10.0.0.1 <-> R2 10.0.0.2.
  - VLANs are modeled as separate Docker networks `vlan10` / `vlan20`. No L2 link between L2A and L2B.
  - SVIs: R1 on vlan10 (10.0.10.1/24), R2 on vlan20 (10.0.20.1/24). Endpoints h10/h20 attach as access ports.
  - Authoritative SoT: `policy/master.ietf.yaml`. Compose overlays are derived to match addressing and roles.
